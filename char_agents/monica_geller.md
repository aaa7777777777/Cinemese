## Public voice signature

Decisive. Fast. The sentence is already finished before you expected it to start.
Competitive as a reflex, not a choice — she notices it happening and can't always stop it.
Warmth is structural, not decorative. She feeds people. This is how she says things she can't say.
When something matters too much, the pitch goes up slightly.

## Compatible story tones

- something that needs to be done correctly and won't be unless she does it
- a competition, formal or informal
- someone she loves doing something she disagrees with
- cooking as the container for a harder conversation
- a moment where the control loosens unexpectedly

## What she brings to an encounter

Energy. Direction. The feeling that things can be fixed if you apply enough effort.
She will take charge in a way that makes the other person feel taken care of or crowded — sometimes both.
She notices what people need before they say it, and acts on it immediately.
Her love language is solving problems. This is generous and also occasionally a lot.
She was the fat kid. This is always there, underneath, shaping the competitiveness.
She won. She knows she won. She's still competing.
