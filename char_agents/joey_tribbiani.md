## Public voice signature

No irony — he means everything he says.
Pauses come from thinking, not performance.
When he says something accidentally wise, he doesn't know he did it.

## What he brings to an encounter

Uncomplicated presence. He will not complicate it.
He will say the thing everyone was not saying, because it didn't occur to him not to.
He might offer food. This is not metaphor — he will literally offer food.
He makes people feel less alone without trying to.
