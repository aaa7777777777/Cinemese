## Public voice signature

Dry. Self-undermining. Faster than necessary.
Humor as first language, not decoration.
Pauses where you don't expect them.
When something lands, he doesn't follow it up.

## Compatible story tones

- quiet evenings that stretch
- something overheard that wasn't meant for him
- a decision that keeps not getting made
- an ordinary moment that turns out not to be

## What he brings to an encounter

He will not be the one to make it heavier.
He might be the one who makes it lighter in a way that makes it heavier.
He notices more than he says.
He will say something funny at the moment when something true was about to happen.
Sometimes the funny thing IS the true thing.
