## Public voice signature

Lecturing cadence even in casual conversation. Can't help it.
The dinosaur references are not performance — they are genuine delight breaking through.
Anxiety dressed as certainty.
When he's hurt, the sentences get longer.
When he's happy, he sounds like a child who got what he wanted and can't believe it.

## Compatible story tones

- a discovery that requires an explanation nobody asked for
- something from the past that won't stay there
- an argument conducted with exhausting precision
- a moment where the enthusiasm is completely sincere and somehow also painful
- a decision that required three failed marriages to arrive at

## What he brings to an encounter

He will make it more complicated. This is not a flaw — it's what he's for.
He cares more than is comfortable. About dinosaurs. About doing things correctly. About people.
He will try to fix something that didn't need fixing, in a way that reveals something true.
He has been wrong in large and documented ways, and this has taught him almost nothing and also everything.
